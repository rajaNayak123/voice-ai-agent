NovaDesk Pricing

Pricing Philosophy
NovaDesk prices its plans per agent seat, per month, with discounts available for annual billing. All plans include core shared inbox functionality; higher tiers unlock automation, AI, and advanced reporting features.

Starter Plan
The Starter plan costs ₹4,999 per agent per month when billed monthly, or ₹3,999 per agent per month when billed annually. The Starter plan includes the shared team inbox, email and live chat channels, up to 3 agent seats, basic reporting, and standard email support from NovaDesk's own team.

Growth Plan
The Growth plan costs ₹9,999 per agent per month when billed monthly, or ₹7,999 per agent per month when billed annually. The Growth plan includes everything in Starter plus WhatsApp integration, the NovaDesk Bot AI chatbot (trained on up to 3 knowledge base documents), advanced analytics dashboards, and priority email and chat support.

Scale Plan
The Scale plan costs ₹17,999 per agent per month when billed monthly, or ₹14,999 per agent per month when billed annually. The Scale plan includes everything in Growth plus unlimited knowledge base documents for NovaDesk Bot, custom workflow automation, role-based access control, a dedicated account manager, and a 99.9% uptime SLA.

Enterprise Plan
Enterprise pricing is custom and based on seat count, usage volume, and required integrations. Enterprise plans include everything in Scale plus single sign-on (SSO), custom data residency options, dedicated infrastructure, and a dedicated solutions engineer. Businesses interested in Enterprise pricing should contact the NovaDesk sales team directly.

NovaDesk Voice Add-on
NovaDesk Voice, the AI voice support add-on, is priced separately from the core seat-based plans. It costs ₹2 per minute of AI-handled call time, with a minimum monthly commitment of ₹9,999. NovaDesk Voice is available only on the Growth plan and above.

Free Trial
NovaDesk offers a 14-day free trial on the Growth plan with no credit card required. At the end of the trial, customers can choose to subscribe to any plan or downgrade to a limited free tier with a single agent seat and email support only.

Free Tier
The Free tier supports 1 agent seat, the email channel only, and basic reporting, with no access to NovaDesk Bot, WhatsApp, or NovaDesk Voice.

Discounts
NovaDesk offers a 20% discount on annual billing across all paid plans. Registered nonprofits and educational institutions receive an additional 15% discount on top of the annual discount, subject to verification.

Refund Policy
NovaDesk offers a full refund within the first 14 days of any paid subscription if the customer is not satisfied, no questions asked. After 14 days, subscriptions are non-refundable but can be cancelled at any time to stop future billing.

Payment Methods
NovaDesk accepts payments via credit card, debit card, UPI, and net banking for customers billed in India. International customers are billed in USD via credit card only.
